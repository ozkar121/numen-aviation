What Is an Empty Leg?

A repositioning
flight. Your opportunity.

When a private jet completes a paid charter, it often needs to fly back empty to its home base — or position for the next client. These are called "empty legs."

Rather than fly empty, operators offer these seats at a steep discount — sometimes up to 75% below the standard charter rate. You get the same aircraft, same service, same experience — just at a fraction of the price.

Availability is limited and changes fast. Contact us directly via WhatsApp to secure a leg before it's gone.
